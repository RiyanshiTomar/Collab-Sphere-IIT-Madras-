**Collab-Sphere** is a collaborative platform designed to help students easily discover and contribute to beginner-friendly open-source projects. Many aspiring developers struggle to find projects that match their skill level — our platform bridges this gap by providing a space to share ideas, connect with peers, and grow together.

---
## 🚀 Problem Statement

Students often struggle to find beginner-friendly open-source projects to contribute to. Existing repositories can be overwhelming or lack guidance, making it difficult for newcomers to get started.

**Collab-Sphere** solves this by offering a student-focused web application where users can share project ideas, discover potential collaborations, and participate in open discussions.

---

## 💡 Key Features

- **👤 User Accounts**: Students can sign up and log in to access all features.
- **📢 Share Ideas**: Users can post project ideas along with the required skillsets.
- **🔍 Explore Ideas**: Browse through project ideas from other users.
- **🤝 Express Interest**: Click the “I’m Interested” button to show intent to collaborate.
- **💬 Project Discussions**: Each project has a dedicated forum for discussions and queries.
- **🛠 Build Projects**: Collaborate and turn ideas into real open-source contributions.
- **🌱 Learn & Grow**: Gain practical experience while contributing to community-led initiatives.

---

## 🖼️ Website Structure

The website consists of the following main sections:

1. **Explore Ideas** – Discover open-source projects to join.
2. **Share Ideas** – Post your own ideas with required skills.
3. **Build Projects** – Team up with peers to develop and contribute.
4. **Discussion Forum** – Interact, solve doubts, and plan collaboration.
5. **Login Page** – Secure access to your dashboard and collaboration tools.
