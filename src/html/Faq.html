Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the 'Sign Up' button in the top navigation. You can register using your GitHub account or email address.

Is Collab-Sphere free to use?

Yes! Our platform is completely free for students and open-source contributors.

What skills do I need to start?

You can start with basic programming knowledge. We have projects for all skill levels from beginner to advanced.

How do I verify my student status?

You can verify using your academic email or upload student ID in your profile settings.

Projects

How do I post a project?

After logging in, click 'Start Project' in your dashboard. Fill in project details and required skills.

Can I join multiple projects?

Yes, you can participate in up to 3 active projects simultaneously.

How are projects moderated?

All projects are reviewed by our team to ensure they meet open-source guidelines and community standards.

Can I monetize my project?

While we encourage open-source projects, you can link to external sponsorship platforms in your project description.
